About Staid.

Staid was created to empower students with the ability to take control of their learning. The tools that Staid provides are meant to aid you, the student, to reach your targets. Reaching your targets is a key goal that Staid aims to achieve, as seen with the logo of Staid being a target. This was created with countless hours of researching various libraries, documentation and more to implement features that work seamlessly with each other. Staid was a great learning opportunity for me to hone my programming skills, including both front-end and back-end in creating this Web App. Everything used here is open-source & you are free to take inspiration from Staid for your projects. If you want to see more of my work or view the repository for this project, you can do so through the footer links.
